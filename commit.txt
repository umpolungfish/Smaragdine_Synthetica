Distillation of the rebis: draw off the volatile essence

REBIS.md goes in the alembic and the pure fraction comes over as
manuscripts/rebis/DISTILLATE.md. Everything contingent stays behind as
caput mortuum: the history of the drafts, the notation table, the
section apparatus, the alchemical witnesses, the full reference list.

What rises is the invariant content in eleven short paragraphs: the
definition of a rebis as an equivalence with the morphism data as the
body; weak conjunction versus strong equivalence with naturality as the
whole difference; the kernel witness in FrobeniusIso.lean; reading and
making as Read/Write with the inverse laws as eXecute; the two
composites that keep the heads distinct; verification available exactly
where time is; isomorphic registers, faces not shadows; the honest
residue as a to-do list of owed naturality squares; and Maria's axiom
completed.

The distillate cites the full body once and nothing else. Nothing in it
is new; that is the point of a distillation. The test is that a reader
of the distillate alone holds every load-bearing claim, and a reader of
both finds no claim in the distillate that the body does not carry.
